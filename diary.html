International Friendship Day Meet Up


Originating from a noble vision, the World Friendship Crusade proposed the very first World Friendship Day on 30th July 1958. This international civil organization has been tirelessly advocating for peace and harmony through the power of friendship. Fast forward to 2011, when the United Nations General Assembly officially declared 30th July as the International Day of Friendship.Since then, this day has become a global celebration, resonating with people from all walks of life.

In today's interconnected world, where social media has transcended boundaries, we have witnessed a surge in the online celebration of World Friendship Day and the International Day of Friendship. People from every corner of the globe come together virtually, sharing messages of camaraderie and spreading the essence of friendship. However, it doesn't end there. We believe that true bonds are forged through personal interactions. That's why we invite you to be a part of our local community activities aimed at bringing individuals from diverse backgrounds together. These activities will provide an opportunity to experience the joy of connecting with new friends, fostering understanding, and building bridges across cultures.

Join us on 30th July as we commemorate the spirit of friendship that unites us all. Together, let's celebrate World Friendship Day and the International Day of Friendship by creating lasting connections and promoting a culture of peace through friendship.

Mark your calendars and stay tuned for further details. We can't wait to see you there!
